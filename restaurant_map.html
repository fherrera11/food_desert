This is a custom food-access score, not an official USDA food desert classification. Higher risk means fewer benchmark-matching food locations near the selected point. Range filters control the scoring model directly. Full matches satisfy every selected range and appear solid purple. Partial matches satisfy all known ranges but have an allowed unknown value, usually missing price or Google rating; they appear solid gray and are flagged in the list.
